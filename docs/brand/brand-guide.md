# Anngel Jacobs Brand Guide

## Brand Direction

Elegant Tech / Founder Engineer / Community Builder

The brand should feel warm, intentional, intelligent, and grounded. It should not feel flashy, crowded, overly trendy, or corporate-cold.

## Visual Personality

- Elegant, but not delicate
- Technical, but not sterile
- Warm, but not casual
- Minimal, but not empty
- Professional, but still human

## Core Message

Anngel builds thoughtful digital systems across engineering, analytics, education, wellness, and community-centered work.

## Tone of Voice

Use language that is clear, calm, and confident.

Good tone:
- Direct
- Helpful
- Reflective
- Professional
- Founder-minded

Avoid:
- Too many buzzwords
- Overly dramatic claims
- Long crowded paragraphs
- Generic portfolio wording

## Typography

### Headings
Preferred: Playfair Display
Fallback: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif

Use for:
- Hero headings
- Section titles
- Project titles
- Resume headline moments

### Body and Interface
Preferred: Manrope
Fallback: Inter, Segoe UI, Arial, sans-serif

Use for:
- Paragraphs
- Navigation
- Buttons
- Forms
- Cards
- Labels

## Color Use

Primary navy should anchor the interface. Warm gold should act as a small signature detail. Cream and ivory should create the soft professional atmosphere.

## Layout Principles

- More whitespace than instinct says
- One strong message per section
- Fewer cards, stronger content
- Clear hierarchy
- Rounded cards and soft borders
- Calm hover states
- Consistent spacing

## UI Components

### Buttons
Primary buttons should be navy with white text.
Secondary buttons should be light with a beige border.

### Cards
Cards should have:
- Soft white or ivory background
- Beige border
- 18px to 24px border radius
- Light shadow only when useful

### Images
Use professional, natural images with clean backgrounds. Prefer photos involving laptop work, studying, engineering, speaking, sports, volunteering, and community.

## Logo Direction

Use a simple wordmark or monogram.

Recommended marks:
- `Anngel`
- `AJ`
- `AJ +`

The logo should feel quiet and timeless. No heavy icon, no complicated symbol, no overly decorative script.

## Website Experience

The site should feel like a personal operating system: portfolio, resume, projects, resources, services, shop, and build log all connected in one polished home.

## Product and Shop Direction

Shop items should match what Anngel actually does:
- Study planners
- SQL and programming guides
- Resume and portfolio templates
- Fitness or sports accountability trackers
- Beginner workout plans
- Digital notebooks
- Engineering study resources

Keep the shop simple, clean, and trustworthy. Every product should solve a real problem.
